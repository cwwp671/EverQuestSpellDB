Tuyen's Chant of Frost

From Project 1999 Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search


A frozen chant that causes between 24 and 40 damage to an enemy and makes them more vulnerable to cold.


Classes

  • Bard - Level 46 (May 1999)

Details

1 : Decrease Hitpoints by 24 per tick (L46) to 31 per tick (L60)
4 : Decrease Cold Resist by 13 (L46) to 17 (L60)
Mana 0 Skill Percussion
Casting Time 3.00 Recast Time 0.00
Fizzle Time 0.00 Resist Cold (0)
Range 200 Target Type Single
Spell Type Detrimental Duration 3 ticks
Cast on You You begin to chant.
Cast on Other Someone begins to chant.
Wears Off Your chant ends.

Items with Spell Effect

  • None
