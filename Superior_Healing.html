Mends severe wounds, healing between 565 and 583 hit points. 2.33 HP/mana; 129.5 HP/sec. Note that this maxes out at 524 hit points (2.096 hp/mana, 116.45 hp/sec) for Druid and Shaman.


Kunark Era Level 53, moved to level 51 for Druids and Shamans a few months after Velious launch [1]. Velious Era Paladins get this spell at 57 during Velious Era


Classes

  • Cleric - Level 34
  • Druid - Level 53 Kunark Era - Level 51 Velious Era
  • Shaman - Level 53 Kunark Era - Level 51 Velious Era
  • Paladin - Level 57 Velious Era
