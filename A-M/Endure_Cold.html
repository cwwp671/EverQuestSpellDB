Endure Cold

From Project 1999 Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Classic Era (added 1999-2000)


Surrounds your target with an aura of protection, warding away cold for 3.0 mins @L1 to 27.0 mins @L9.


Classes

  • Cleric - Level 14
  • Ranger - Level 22
  • Shadow Knight - Level 15
  • Druid - Level 9
  • Shaman - Level 1
  • Necromancer - Level 4

Details

1 : Increase Cold Resist by 11 (L1) to 20 (L10)
Mana 20 Skill Abjuration
Casting Time 2.50 Recast Time 2.25
Fizzle Time 2.25 Resist Unresistable
Range 100 Target Type Single
Spell Type Beneficial Duration 3 minutes @L1 to 27 minutes @L9
Cast on You You feel protected from cold.
Cast on Other Someone is protected from cold.
Wears Off Your endurance to cold fades.

Items with Spell Effect

  • Potion of Heat
