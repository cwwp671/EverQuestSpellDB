Fungal Regrowth

From Project 1999 Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

You experience the regrowth of fungal spores.


Classes

This spell is cast by items only.

Note: The snare effect is only present in cast versions of this spell, such as from the fungi staves; the fungi tunic's worn effect does not reduce your movement speed.

Additional note: The item-cast buff does NOT land if you have any movement speed buffs active, and overrides, and is overwritten by Regrowth

Details

1 : Increase Hitpoints by 5 per tick (L1) to 15 per tick (L20)
2 : Decrease Movement Speed by 10% (L1) to 42% (L65)
Mana 100 Skill
Casting Time 6.00 Recast Time 0.00
Fizzle Time 2.50 Resist Unresistable
