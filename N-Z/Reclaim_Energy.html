Reclaim Energy

From Project 1999 Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search
Classic Era (added 1999-2000)

Reclaims the energy needed to maintain your pet, causing the pet to dissipate and restores the mana used to cast the pet times the current health percentage the pet is at (or very close to it).


Classes

  • Necromancer - Level 1
  • Magician - Level 1
  • Enchanter - Level 1

Details

1 : Reclaim Energy
Mana 5 Skill Conjuration
Casting Time 2.50 Recast Time 2.25
Fizzle Time 2.25 Resist Unresistable
Range 100 Target Type Pet
Spell Type Beneficial Duration Instant
Cast on You
Cast on Other Someone disperses.
Wears Off
